Vinovest has taken a passion for rare wine and turned it into an
alternative investment opportunity.
Now the world’s leading wine investment platform, Vinovest provides
opportunities for even the most novice investors to purchase rare wines
from all over the globe. (Think Robin Hood meets Stock X meets BitCoin)
Vinovest experienced fast success, but they had outgrown their website.
They had new features that needed to be rolled out quickly within their
application to improve the user experience.
That’s where
Bean Machine came in
Through strategic project management and efficient
development, we collaborated with Vinovest’s internal
designers to bring the Vinovest marketing site into a full
React JS based web application, integrating Contentful
to provide a custom workflow that allows for scalability.
